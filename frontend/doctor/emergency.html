Declare a Doctor Emergency

Use this form when an emergency prevents you from attending a scheduled appointment. The affected patient will be automatically notified and their appointment rescheduled to the next available day.

Emergency Details
Important: This action will block the slot and auto-reschedule any existing appointment. This cannot be undone.
What Happens Next?
1
The emergency slot is blocked immediately — no new bookings can be made for that date and time.
2
If an appointment already exists for that slot, it is automatically rescheduled to the next day.
3
The patient receives an in-portal notification explaining the reschedule.
